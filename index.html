Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

What is the Umurabyo Loan? ↓
The Umurabyo Loan is a short-term cash loan that helps clients cover emergencies until their next payday. It is repaid within one month.
How long does the approval process take? ↓
The approval process is quick and efficient. Once your application is submitted, we aim to provide a decision within 24 hours.
How much can I borrow with the Umurabyo Loan? ↓
You can borrow up to 500,000 Frw depending on your eligibility and repayment capacity.
Are there any hidden fees? ↓
No, there are no hidden fees. We only charge a small monthly interest on the loan amount.
How do I repay the loan? ↓
Repayment is simple. You can repay the loan in monthly installments through various payment methods. We will send you reminders before your due date.
Can I reloan after paying back my loan? ↓
Yes! Once you have paid off your loan, you are eligible to apply for another loan under the same terms.
