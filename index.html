Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

What is the Umurabyo Loan? ↓
The Umurabyo Loan is a short-term cash loan that helps clients cover emergencies until their next payday. It is repaid within one month.
How much can I borrow from Kigali Microloans? ↓
You can borrow up to 500,000 Rwandan Francs (Frw) for Umurabyo Loan.
What is the repayment period? ↓
The repayment period for the Umurabyo Loan is one month. You are expected to pay back the loan in full by the end of the month.
How long does the approval process take? ↓
The approval process is very quick, usually taking 1-2 business days.
What happens if I miss a repayment? ↓
If you miss a repayment, you will incur a late fee. Please contact us to discuss your options if you're having trouble making your payment.
Are there any hidden fees? ↓
No, there are no hidden fees. We charge a small monthly interest rate, which is clearly stated at the time of your loan agreement.
