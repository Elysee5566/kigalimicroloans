About Kigali MicroLoans

At Kigali MicroLoans, we empower individuals in Rwanda with fast, accessible personal loans. We know life can bring unexpected challenges, so we're here to support you through financial emergencies with fast, reliable services.

Founded in 2020, we focus on helping you navigate urgent needs, from medical expenses to school fees, with minimal paperwork and no collateral.

Our Mission

To be Rwanda’s most trusted, neighborhood lender, offering quick, life-changing financial support to clients when they need it most.

Our Values

  • Trust: Providing transparent, reliable services builds strong client relationships.
  • Customer Focus: We prioritize our clients’ needs with personalized, caring support.
  • Integrity: Upholding the highest ethical standards ensures our clients’ trust and respect.
